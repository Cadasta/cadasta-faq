Changing a Basemap for a Collector Map

  1. FAQ
  2. Editing how the map looks
  3. Changing a Basemap for a Collector Map

Purpose

This page will explain how to change a basemap for a web map in ArcGIS Online (AGOL)

Requirements

  • You will need to have a username and password set up for you by a Cadasta team member. If you do not have a user please contact support@cadasta.org
  • You will need to sign to your account - see Introduction to your account for a reminder

Steps

  1. In AGOL, navigate to your Group page

  2. Press the "Content" tab in your Group page

  3. Find and select your Collector Map (Organization Name)

  4. Press the "Open in Map Viewer" button

  5. See the Map Viewer with the Collector Map (Organization Name)

  6. Press the "Basemap" button

  7. Choose your desired Basemap Note see the difference in the two Basemaps

  8. Press the "Save" button
