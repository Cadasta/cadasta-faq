Adding a drone image to AGOL/Portal

  1. FAQ
  2. Adding data
  3. Adding a drone image to AGOL/Portal

Purpose

This page will explain how to upload an orthomosaic file to your account on ArcGIS Online (AGOL)

Requirements

  • You will need to have a username and password set up for you by a Cadasta team member. If you do not have a user please contact support@cadasta.org
  • You will need to sign to your account - see Introduction to your account for a reminder
  • You will need to have an Esri license for ArcMap or ArcGIS Pro and have it installed on your workstation

Steps

  1. Open ArcMap or ArcGIS Pro
  2. Open the drone image

  3. Right click the image layer and select Share -> as Web Layer

  4. Select “Map Image” option

  5. Go to the “Configuration” tab and then select the pencil icon next to the image layer

  6. Make sure to slide the “levels of detail” to its maximum value (level 23)
